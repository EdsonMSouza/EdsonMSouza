### Hi, I'm Dr. Edson Melo de Souza 

I'm Full Professor at Universidade Nove de Julho - UNINOVE located in São Paulo - Brazil.

Postdoctoral fellow (2025) and Ph.D. (2021) in Informatics and Knowledge Management, M.Sc. in Production Engineering (2013), and B.Sc. in Computer Science (2005), all from Universidade Nove de Julho (UNINOVE). Develops interdisciplinary research in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Metascience, and Knowledge Management. Currently a permanent faculty member of the Graduate Program in Informatics and Knowledge Management (PPGI/UNINOVE) and Project Management (PPGP/UNINOVE), advising master’s and doctoral students and participating in applied research projects at national and international levels.
